  • It's late 17th century - the Golden Age of Piracy!

    From the western Atlantic, around the southern tip of Africa on to India lays a sailing route - the Pirate Round. Pirates who venture into the Indian Ocean are usually met with great fortune, but also with painful cannon shots as the British East India Company ships get better armed escorts. Will pirates still be plundering vessels as they go? Or can they be stopped?

    Become part of one side of the war and find out who is in charge of the seas!
